# Third Party Tools

## wechat-article-parser

- Source: https://github.com/huanjuedadehen/wechat-article-parser
- License: MIT
- Usage: dependency, not vendored source.
- Role: parse `mp.weixin.qq.com` article links into metadata and Markdown.

This parser can still be limited by WeChat verification, rate limiting, or article permissions. MyStand Parser Tools treats those as structured errors instead of guessing article content.
